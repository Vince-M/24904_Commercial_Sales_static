About Commercial Sales

What started as a two-person operation coordinating select special orders has now evolved into a full-scale team managing B2B sales across Canada.

Our customers frequently include:

  • Band offices
  • Hamlets/Towns
  • School Boards and Education Authorities
  • Health Authorities
  • Tribal Councils
  • Land Holdings and Advocacy Groups
  • Government Organizations

Our team includes dedicated sales representatives to work with you from start to finish in the procurement process. We do a full needs assessment including budget, timelines, and product selection.

Once a clear picture of your needs is established, our skilled administration team takes over, sourcing products from our vast vendor supply network to ensure you receive the best pricing possible. A detailed quote is delivered to your inbox for review, and our team is at the ready to answer any questions or make adjustments as needed.

Once satisfied with your quote, you can sit back and relax as we handle everything on the logistics end - consider us your very own “easy button”. Your order will be procured, packaged, and shipped to your door without you lifting a finger. Not only is the process seamless, but you also take advantage of our preferred shipping rates with many northern airlines and freight services. Our sealift shipping in particular offer phenomenal savings, as our NEAS partnership (Nunavut Eastern Arctic Shipping) allows you access to some of the lowest barge rates in the country.
